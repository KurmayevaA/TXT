A software tester needs the following skills:

1 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
2 Attention to detail
3 Good communication and collaboration skills
4 Knowledge of software testing principles and methodologies
5 Ability to write clear and concise test cases
6 Familiarity with testing tools and technologies
7 Understanding of software development processes and lifecycle
8 Ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines
9 Adaptability and willingness to learn new technologies and tools
10Strong organizational and time-management skills.
These skills are essential for a software tester to ensure that software applications are thoroughly tested and meet the requirements and expectations of users.
